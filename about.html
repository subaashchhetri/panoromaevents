Who We Are

Elevating Nepalgunj's Event Culture

Panorama Events Pvt. Ltd. was founded in Nepalgunj with a simple vision: to bring top-tier national music productions and live entertainment to Western Nepal. Historically, massive concerts were confined to Kathmandu, but we wanted to prove that Nepalgunj can host shows of equal—if not superior—energy, sound, and lighting quality.

With 6+ successful large-scale concerts organized under our belt, we have hosted iconic bands like Cobweb, Abhaya and the Steam Engines, Sabin Rai & The Pharaoh, Pariwartan, and Swopna Suman, establishing ourselves as Banke district's most trusted name in live music events.

From ticketing and artist logistics to sound engineering and stage setup, our team executes every event with professional discipline and attention to detail.

Nepali artists delivering high-voltage performances on our stage

Our Mission

To bridge the musical gap by hosting top-tier concerts and festivals in Nepalgunj, offering high-fidelity audio-visual experiences for the local audience while supporting native artists and organizations.

Our Vision

To establish Nepalgunj as the major live entertainment hub of Western Nepal, creating opportunities for cross-regional tours, brand partnerships, and community celebrations through music.
